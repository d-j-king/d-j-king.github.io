Daniel King

AI & energy policy · Research Fellow, Foundation for American Innovation

I'm a research fellow at the Foundation for American Innovation, where I work on physical infrastructure for artificial intelligence — data centers, grid modernization, and power generation. I've published and testified on data center buildout, electric grid reform, and behind-the-meter generation.

I write Policy Gradients, a newsletter making sense of the AI and energy landscape. Before FAI, I worked at a power generation startup.

I hold an MA in statistics and data science from Yale and a BS in mechanical engineering and applied mathematics from Brown.
